# Playku — Support

Playku (玩龄) is an offline, no-ads, no-subscription daily play guide for parents of toddlers aged 12–36 months. Below are answers to common questions. If you don't find what you need, email **luanlzsn@gmail.com** or contact the developer via the App Store listing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How are today's activities chosen?
They are picked for your child's exact age in months and balanced across the five developmental areas (fine motor, language, cognitive, gross motor, social-emotional). Add your child's birthday during onboarding, or later in **Profile → Edit**, so the picks fit.

### Is there a subscription?
No — never. Playku is a one-time purchase. Buy once and keep everything forever, with no auto-renewal and no ads. You can also unlock individual area or age packs à la carte.

### I bought it on another device or reinstalled — how do I get my content back?
Open **Profile → Restore purchases**, signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy it. Your unlock restores for free.

### Does Playku need the internet or an account?
No. The app works fully offline with no sign-in. Your child's information stays on your device only.

### Which languages are supported?
English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, Español, 日本語, Deutsch and 한국어. Playku follows your device language automatically.

### How do I mark an activity as played, or save a favorite?
On an activity, tap **We played today** to log it, or the **heart** to save it. Your logs and favorites appear under the **Records** tab.

### My child seems ahead of / behind the milestones — should I worry?
Milestones in Playku are gentle observations, not a score, and every child grows at their own pace. Playku never compares your child to anyone. For any real concern about development, health, or behavior, please consult a pediatrician.

### Where is my data stored? How do I delete it?
Everything (your child's profile, play logs, favorites, milestone notes) is stored only on your device. There are no servers. To erase all local data, simply delete the app.
